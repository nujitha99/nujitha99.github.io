About
A little about who I am and what drives me

Hey, I'm Nujitha — a software engineer with a research edge, currently based in Toronto, Ontario. I hold an MSc in Computer Science from Brock University (95%) and a First Class BEng in Software Engineering from the University of Westminster, London.

My engineering background spans production backend systems at IFS — building microservices for Aviation & Defence ERP platforms serving 100+ global clients — and real-time airline operations software at Avtra, deployed across international airports. I've worked across the full stack: Java Spring Boot backends, Angular frontends, cloud deployments on AWS/GCP/Azure, and CI/CD automation end-to-end.

Alongside engineering, I've done genuine research at Brock University — building ML-based intrusion detection systems for vehicular networks, publishing three peer-reviewed papers in IEEE and Elsevier venues, and presenting at international conferences. My work sits at the intersection of security, distributed systems, and applied ML. Not all engineers publish. I happen to do both.

I've also taught. As a Teaching Assistant at Brock, I ran lab sessions, gave 1-on-1 debugging help, and explained hard things to people who were stuck. That's a skill I bring into teams too — I write clear docs, give useful code reviews, and don't hoard knowledge.
